[ROW:010]
Source: CRR Article 26(1)(a)
Text:
Ordinary shares issued by the institution qualify as Common Equity Tier 1 capital.

[ROW:020]
Source: CRR Article 26(1)(b)
Text:
Share premium related to Common Equity Tier 1 instruments qualifies as Common Equity Tier 1 capital.

[ROW:030]
Source: CRR Article 26(1)(c)
Text:
Retained earnings qualify as Common Equity Tier 1 capital.

[ROW:060]
Source: CRR Article 26(1)
Text:
Common Equity Tier 1 capital is the sum of eligible Common Equity Tier 1 items after regulatory adjustments.
